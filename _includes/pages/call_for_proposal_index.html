Call for Proposal

GopherConIndia 2017, taking place for the third time in India, is accepting all types of talk proposals upto 9:30 pm IST on November 30th, 2016. Due to the competitive nature of the selection process, we encourage prospective speakers to submit their proposals as early as possible. We're looking forward to receiving your best proposals for talks.

Anyone with any level of Go knowledge is a candidate for a great topic at this conference. As we will get attendees of all kinds, we need speakers of all kinds. In all ways and manners, we will try to assemble the most diverse conference we can, and we do that with your help.

These are the traditional talk sessions given during the main conference days, Friday through Saturday. All talks are 25 minutes long and includes Qs. and Ans.

As for the topic, that's up to you. We want something for everyone. We want the hot topics in the community right now. We also want the hot topics for the future. Where is Go going? You tell us. You're the people who make those decisions and we want to share them.

GopherConIndia is entirely volunteer-organized and run, and works hard to keep costs as low as possible.

As such:

  • selected speakers will have to spend on and make their own travel, boarding and lodging arrangements.
  • there is no talk remuneration to any speaker whether he/she is an invited / selected foreign or Indian speaker.

Finally do note that:

  • we are accepting only single participant talks.
  • you can submit as many talk proposals as you want.
  • we require all selected talks to comply wholly with our Code of Conduct Policy. If you have any questions at all about this, don't hesitate to ask us at team@emergingtechs.in.
  • every proposal submission will be responded to, whether or not the talk is accepted.
  • all the speakers are picked based on their involvement in the community and contribution to Go and in no way reflects the bias towards any speakers. The speakers submit the application and is carefully reviewed by the board to approve them as speakers.
  • if your talk is selected, we will need your:
    • draft presentation latest by 25th Jan. 2017 for review.
    • talk slides latest by 10th Feb. 2017.

You don't need to be a well traveled professional speaker to get on stage at GopherConIndia. What matters is the topics, the material, and the drive to present great things to this community.

Whether or not your talk is accepted, if you submit a proposal, we'll reserve a ticket space for you. That means that if you're waiting to purchase a spot just in case your proposal is accepted, you don't need to worry. Even if the conference sells out and your proposals aren't accepted, you'll still have the chance to buy a GopherConIndia ticket.
