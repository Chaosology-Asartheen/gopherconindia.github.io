Go Workshops

GopherConIndia 2017 is proud to work with Ardan Labs to bring to you two full day Go workshops (first one for beginners and the second for seasoned developers):

  • The Ultimate Go Workshop on Wednesday, 22nd Feb. 2017.
  • The Advanced Go Workshop on Thursday, 23rd Feb. 2017.

Note: The proceeds of the Go workshop will go towards part-funding the conference.

A total of only 20 participants for each Go workshop.

Who teaches this course?

William Kennedy is a managing partner at Ardan Studio in Miami, Florida, a mobile, web, and systems development company. He is also a co-author of the book Go in Action, the author of the blog GoingGo.Net, and a founding member of GoBridge which is working to increase Go adoption through diversity.
