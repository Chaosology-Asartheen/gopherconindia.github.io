Venue

Bangalore is the most sought out cosmopolitan city in India and best known for making people all over the world feel at home.

Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India is the ideal location for our first Indian conference. With being host to all global software companies for many many years, Bangalore boasts some of the most world-class restaurants and great nightlife to round out your conference downtime.

No wonder, its termed as Software and Party Hub of India. What a way to experience this along with Bangalore hosting a Go Conference for the first time in India.
